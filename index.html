Cole Bruner, a missionary true, Takes a bite of blossoms, sweet and new. In the garden bright, he takes a seat, But watch out for bees, oh so fleet!

With petals on his lips, he smiles so wide, Takes another bite, with a joyful stride. But a buzzing bee might just step aside, Will Cole be stung, or will he survive?

Only the blossoms know, without fail, As Cole savors each bite, with a happy tale!
